Stock market prediction model using LASSO Regression applied to NSE NIFTY50 data. Technical indicators — Moving Averages, RSI, and Super Trend — combined with time-lag features capture sequential price patterns, while LASSO handles both feature selection and regularisation on a limited initial dataset.

Achieved a Test RMSE of 14.78 and MAPE of 2.98% on held-out NIFTY50 data. Methodology peer-reviewed and published in IEEE.
